F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can you provide custom-designed plastic packaging solutions to meet our unique branding and product requirements?

    Yes, we specialize in customized plastic packaging solutions. We can create customized moulds solely designed to cater towards manufacturing packaging for your brand's products.

  • Do you offer printing or labeling services for branding and product information on the packaging?

    No, we do not offer printing services. However, we do provide labelling services for any product.

  • What kind of material do you use to produce your plastic packaging?

    We use a variety of materials such as PE, PP, LDPE, and PET to produce our plastic packaging.

  • What is your minimum and maximum order size? How long do you take to manufacture the products?

    Our minimum order size is 5,000 and our maximum order size is 25,000. The time period to produce a bulk order varies according to the order size. An estimation would be around 1 to 2 weeks.

  • Are your plastic packaging materials compliant with Ghanaian safety and environmental regulations?

    Yes, our materials meet all relevant safety and environmental regulations. Our plastic packaging solution are well-known for being eco-friendly and food-grade.

  • Do you pack and deliver products to your customers warehouses or stores?

    No, we do not directly deliver products to our customers warehouses/stores. However, we do pack our products into PE sacks for customers to pickup from our location.
